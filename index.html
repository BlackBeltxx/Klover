About

Building a disciplined investment platform.

Klover Capital Group is a Singapore-incorporated investment holding company with a partner presence across Singapore, Hong Kong, and New York. The group is focused on developing institutional-grade governance, operational infrastructure, and cross-border capabilities across Asian and global financial markets.

The group has established subsidiary entities in Singapore and Hong Kong as part of its organisational development. Partners are based across multiple locations to support the group's international orientation.

Our founding partners bring combined experience across capital markets, asset management, family office services, and institutional investment spanning Asia, the United States, and global markets.

Group Structure

A Singapore-based holding company with an international partner network.

Klover Capital Group is organised under a Singapore holding company, with wholly owned subsidiary entities in Singapore and Hong Kong, and a founding partner based in New York.

The group's structure is designed to support its founding partners' cross-border experience and long-term organisational objectives.
