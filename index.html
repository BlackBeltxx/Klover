About

A modern platform for families that prefer judgment over noise.

Klover Capital Group is a Singapore-based investment platform built for family offices and sophisticated investors seeking trusted access to cross-border opportunities.

The firm combines institutional discipline with entrepreneurial flexibility, allowing investors to participate selectively through co-investments and specialized vehicles across public and private markets.
