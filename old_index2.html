About

A disciplined platform under development.

Klover Capital Group is a Singapore-based investment holding and strategic research group focused on cross-border capital markets, private markets, and technology-enabled investment processes.

The group is currently developing its internal governance framework, research capabilities, and operating infrastructure in preparation for future regulated activities, subject to applicable regulatory approvals.

Strategic Focus

Research-led areas of strategic interest.

Capital Markets Research

Monitoring public market developments, IPO activity, equity capital markets, and corporate events across selected Asian and global markets.

Private Markets Research

Studying late-stage private companies, growth sectors, and long-term structural themes across technology, healthcare, consumer, and industrial sectors.

Special Situations

Reviewing corporate actions, restructurings, secondary transactions, and other market events from a research and strategy development perspective.

AI & Quant Infrastructure

Developing data-driven research tools, quantitative analysis frameworks, and technology-enabled workflows to support internal investment analysis.
